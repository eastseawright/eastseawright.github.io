AI Battery Assistant Chatbot

An intelligent conversational AI system built with Microsoft Copilot Studio that helps customers instantly find battery specifications and product information, informed by user journey analytics to optimize the knowledge base.

Role UX/UI Applications Intern
Timeline 2024
Company Clarios
Microsoft Copilot Studio Large Language Models Hotjar Analytics Conversational AI

Overview

Customers visiting Clarios' website often struggled to find the right battery specifications for their vehicles. Traditional search and navigation required knowing technical details and navigating through multiple product pages. The challenge was to create an intuitive, conversational interface that could understand natural language queries and provide accurate product recommendations instantly.

The Problem

Complex Product Catalog

Thousands of battery SKUs with varying specifications made traditional search inefficient for customers.

User Journey Friction

Analytics showed high drop-off rates when customers couldn't quickly find compatible products.

Knowledge Accessibility

Product information existed but wasn't surfaced in a way that matched how customers actually asked questions.

Solution Approach

01

Copilot Studio Implementation

Built a conversational AI chatbot using Microsoft Copilot Studio that understands natural language queries about battery specifications, compatibility, and features. The bot can handle complex questions like "What battery do I need for a 2018 Honda Civic?" and provide accurate recommendations.

02

Data-Driven Knowledge Base

Used Hotjar to analyze customer journey data and identify the most common questions and pain points. This analysis directly informed which information to prioritize in the LLM's knowledge base, ensuring the chatbot could answer the questions customers actually asked.

03

Contextual Understanding

Configured the chatbot to maintain conversation context, allowing for follow-up questions and clarifications. Customers can refine their search through natural dialogue rather than starting over.

04

Integration & Handoff

Designed seamless handoff to human support when the bot encounters queries outside its knowledge base, ensuring customers always get answers.

Technical Implementation

Conversational Design

Created dialogue flows that feel natural and helpful, not robotic. Designed prompts that guide users while allowing flexibility in how they phrase questions.

Knowledge Base Architecture

Structured product data and FAQs in a way optimized for LLM retrieval. Implemented metadata tagging and semantic relationships to improve answer relevance.

Analytics Integration

Connected Hotjar session recordings and heatmaps to identify where users struggled in their journey. This qualitative data drove knowledge base prioritization decisions.

Impact & Metrics

Monthly Interactions

The chatbot significantly improved the customer experience by providing instant, accurate answers to battery-related questions. The data-driven approach to knowledge base optimization ensured high relevance and utility from launch.

Key Learnings

  • User Research is Critical: Hotjar analytics revealed customer needs that weren't obvious from product data alone. Real user behavior should drive AI training priorities.
  • Conversation Design Complexity: Creating natural dialogue flows requires anticipating many edge cases and gracefully handling ambiguous queries.
  • Continuous Improvement: LLM-based chatbots improve over time with more data. Built systems to monitor unsuccessful queries and expand the knowledge base accordingly.
  • Trust Through Transparency: Users appreciated when the bot acknowledged limitations and offered human handoff rather than providing uncertain answers.
