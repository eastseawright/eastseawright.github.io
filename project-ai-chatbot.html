AI Battery Assistant

An AI agent built with Microsoft Copilot Studio that helps customers instantly find battery warranty and product information. Instead of searching through web pages for 5+ minutes, customers get a specific answer in about 20 seconds.

Role UX/UI Applications Intern
Company Clarios
Microsoft Copilot Studio Large Language Models Hotjar Analytics Conversational AI

Overview

Customers visiting Clarios' Optima battery website had a hard time finding warranty information. The process took 5+ minutes of clicking through pages, and most people ended up on a generic contact page without an answer. That frustration was costing the company sales.

I built an AI agent that gives customers specific warranty answers in about 20 seconds — a 93% reduction in lookup time.

The Problem

Too Many Clicks

Finding warranty info took 5+ minutes of navigating through multiple pages, causing frustration and drop-off.

Hard to Find Answers

Important warranty details were buried deep in documentation that most customers never found.

Lost Sales

Customers who couldn't quickly confirm warranty coverage often gave up or went to a competitor.

Solution Approach

01

Warranty Info Front and Center

Made warranty information the agent's #1 priority since that's what customers were struggling to find. The bot helps users get exact warranty details for their specific product, which directly impacts purchase decisions.

02

Plain English Conversations

Built with Microsoft Copilot Studio so customers can ask questions naturally — like "What's the warranty on the Optima RedTop?" — and get a specific answer instead of being sent to a generic contact page.

03

Built Around Real Customer Questions

Uploaded warranty manuals and product guides to the bot's knowledge base. Used Google Analytics to find out what customers actually searched for most, then prioritized those topics so the bot could handle the most common questions first.

04

On-Brand Design

Designed a clean bot icon and chat interface that matches Clarios' brand. The goal was to make it feel like a natural part of the website, not an intrusive pop-up.

Technical Implementation

Conversation Design

Created dialogue flows that feel natural and helpful. Designed prompts that guide users while letting them phrase questions however they want.

Knowledge Base Setup

Organized product data and FAQs so the AI can find the right answer quickly. Tagged content with relevant categories to improve accuracy.

User Behavior Analysis

Used Hotjar session recordings and heatmaps to see exactly where customers got stuck on the website. That real user data guided which topics to prioritize in the agent.
